## The Core Philosophy: "The Machine That Thinks With You"

Before any design decision, you need **one sentence** that your entire website is built around. Based on what Crux actually does:

> **"Your goal. Your agents. Done."**

Apple sells empowerment. Claude sells safety. Perplexity sells truth. **Crux should sell delegation** — the feeling that you just hired a team that never sleeps. [1]

***

## Emotional Architecture (The Page Flow)

Every section must trigger a specific emotion in sequence. This is how Apple, Perplexity and Claude do it — they engineer feelings, not just layouts. [2]

```
Section 1 → RECOGNITION    "That's exactly my problem"
Section 2 → AWE            "Wait... it can do THAT?"
Section 3 → TRUST          "Serious people use this"
Section 4 → EXCITEMENT     "I want this NOW"
Section 5 → SAFETY         "It's okay to sign up"
```

***

## Section-by-Section Blueprint

### Hero (Recognition + Awe)
- **Dark background** — signals premium, serious, intelligent [3]
- **One large animated headline** that cycles through real user goals:
  - *"Research my competitor's pricing"*
  - *"Design a character for my screenplay"*
  - *"Write and send 40 investor emails"*
- Subheadline: **"Crux builds the agents. Agents do the work."**
- One CTA button: **"Start Free — No card needed"**
- Below the fold: a 15-second auto-playing loop of a real session running — agents spinning up, tasks completing, files downloading

This directly mirrors what Perplexity does with their Computer landing page — show the product working, not just describe it. [4]

### Problem Section (Recognition — India-specific)
This is your India unlock. Indian users respond deeply to **"wasted effort" framing** — the frustration of doing manually what a machine should do. [5]

> *"You spend 3 hours researching. 2 hours writing. 1 hour formatting. Crux does all three in 8 minutes."*

Show a split — left side: a person at a laptop exhausted at 2am. Right side: Crux session completing the same task while they sleep.

### How It Works (Awe)
Not a feature list — a **live demo walkthrough** in 3 steps with real output previews. Show the Character Design workflow from our earlier conversation — it's a perfect visual story: one prompt → 5 agents → full character bible downloaded as DOCX.

### Trust Section (Trust)
- Real session token counts + cost ($0.14 for 10-agent session)
- Agent names running in real-time with live status dots
- "Powered by Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini — you choose"
- Logos of all model providers [6]

### Use Cases Grid (Excitement)
Not generic. Real, specific, dramatic use cases that Indian users connect with:
- 🎬 *Screenwriters* — character bibles in minutes
- 📊 *Startup founders* — investor research + cold emails
- 🎓 *Students* — deep research + formatted reports
- 💼 *Freelancers* — proposal writing + client research
- 🏢 *Agencies* — content pipelines on autopilot

### Pricing (Safety)
Single CTA, no confusion. India-specific psychological insight: **free tier must feel generous**, not crippled. Indian users test deeply before paying. [6]

- Free: 5 sessions/day, all models
- Pro ₹499/month: Unlimited sessions, document generation, priority
- No credit card on free tier — this is non-negotiable for India conversion

## Visual Design Language

| Element | Decision | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Background** | Near-black `#0A0A0F` | Premium, Claude-level credibility |
| **Accent color** | Electric blue-violet `#6B4FFF` | Signals intelligence, not aggression |
| **Typography** | Inter or Geist — large, bold headlines | Apple-level clarity [7] |
| **Animations** | Subtle particle field in hero, smooth scroll reveals | Perplexity-level polish [4] |
| **Agent UI** | Show actual running agent cards from your real UI | Trust through authenticity |
| **Mobile** | Design mobile-first — 80%+ India traffic is mobile | Non-negotiable for India [6] |

***

## India-Specific Emotional Triggers

Indian users are uniquely motivated by three things that most Western AI sites completely ignore: [5]

1. **Jugaad pride** — *"Do more with less"* — frame Crux as the ultimate jugaad: one person with Crux = a team of 10
2. **Status aspiration** — *"This is what top professionals use"* — show it as aspirational, not just useful
3. **Value anxiety** — show cost-per-task: *"₹2 to research, write, and format a full report"* — this converts Indian users faster than any feature list

***

## What Competitors Miss (Your Advantage)

| Brand | What They Do Well | What They Miss |
|---|---|---|
| **Claude** | Trust, safety messaging | Doesn't show the work happening |
| **Perplexity** | Beautiful, fast, credible | Single-agent only, no delegation story |
| **OpenClaw** | Technical credibility | Cold, no emotional narrative for India |
| **Manus** | Clean task demo | No agent transparency, black box |

**Crux's unique angle:** You can show the entire agent team thinking out loud — agent names, tasks, tokens, real outputs. No competitor shows this level of transparency. Make that your superpower. Every section should reinforce: *"You can see exactly what's happening. Nothing is hidden."*

## Tech Stack for the Site

Since you're building this in parallel on another account:

- **Next.js 14** — App Router, SSR for SEO
- **Tailwind + Framer Motion** — animations without bloat [8]
- **Lottie** — for the agent running animation in hero
- **Vercel** — deploy instantly, edge CDN for India latency
- **Hotjar** — track where Indian users drop off from day one

Build it as a standalone repo — zero dependency on the main platform. It just needs one API call to start a session on your backend when someone clicks "Start Free."
